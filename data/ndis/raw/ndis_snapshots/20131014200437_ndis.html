    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 10,535,300 offender1 profiles, 1,613,100 arrestee profiles and 509,900 forensic profiles as of August 2013. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of August 2013, CODIS has produced over 219,700 hits assisting in more than 210,700 investigations.
