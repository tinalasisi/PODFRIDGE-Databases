    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success

    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 9,965,486 offender profiles and 384,604 forensic profiles as of July 2011. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS's primary metric, the "Investigation Aided," tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of July 2011, CODIS has produced over 149,200 hits assisting in more than 143,200 investigations.
