    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 12,163,600 offender1 profiles, 2,228,956 arrestee profiles and 679,446 forensic profiles as of January 2016. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of January 2016, CODIS has produced over 318,266 hits assisting in more than 305,942 investigations.
