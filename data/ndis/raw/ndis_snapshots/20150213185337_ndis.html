    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 11,548,720 offender1 profiles, 1,303,454 arrestee profiles and 601,664 forensic profiles as of December 2014. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of December 2014, CODIS has produced over 270,326 hits assisting in more than 258,168 investigations.
