    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 10,971,392 offender1 profiles, 1,892,952 arrestee profiles and 559,705 forensic profiles as of May 2014. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of May 2014, CODIS has produced over 245,447 hits assisting in more than 235,540 investigations.
