    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 11,962,222 offender1 profiles, 2,120,729 arrestee profiles and 657,298 forensic profiles as of September 2015. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of September 2015, CODIS has produced over 296,490 hits assisting in more than 282,175 investigations.
