    NDIS Statistics

    CODIS—NDIS Statistics

    Measuring Success
    The National DNA Index (NDIS) contains over 11,822,927 offender1 profiles, 2,028,734 arrestee profiles and 638,162 forensic profiles as of June 2015. Ultimately, the success of the CODIS program will be measured by the crimes it helps to solve. CODIS’s primary metric, the “Investigation Aided,” tracks the number of criminal investigations where CODIS has added value to the investigative process. As of June 2015, CODIS has produced over 288,298 hits assisting in more than 274,648 investigations.
